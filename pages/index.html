FNF Kaeya

FNF Kaeya is a dope ass rhythm game.

This is a Genshin Impact mod of Friday Night Funkin' (FNF). Features Kaeya and Keqing as well as playable Genshin songs.

Controls

  • WASD / Arrow keys: Move
  • - and +: Change volume
  • 0: Mute
  • Enter: Confirm selection (or pause during a level)
  • ESC: Go to previous menu

The game pauses if you click outside the page. Just click the game again to resume.
